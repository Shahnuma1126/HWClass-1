Behind Fiverr’s New B2B Campaign: “Team Up”

By Fiverr Team | August 9, 2022

Fiverr’s latest campaign, called "Team Up," celebrates freelancers as an extension of your team.‍

With so much change in the air, an increasing number of companies are flexing their in-house teams with freelance talent to increase their adaptability, productivity and manage scalability. 93% of a recent Deloitte and MIT Sloane Management survey’s participants consider external contributors (including freelancers) an integral part of their workforce. 91% say their organization supports managers looking to hire external workers. And finding the perfect match has never been easier.

The hardest part? You might miss them once they finish the job.

But what do these independent workers look like? "Team Up" brings a freelancer and their temporary business team together. It’s Fiverr’s first campaign that puts a larger focus on medium to large-sized businesses, showing how they can incorporate freelancers into their teams to successfully tackle any initiative.
